# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Blueprint Engine is built around collaboration, creativity, and responsible innovation.

We want a community where contributors can share ideas, improvements, and feedback respectfully.

## Expected Behavior

Community members should:

- Be respectful
- Provide constructive feedback
- Share knowledge
- Respect different approaches
- Focus on improving the project

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples include:

- Harassment
- Personal attacks
- Discrimination
- Malicious contribution attempts
- Deliberate disruption

## Reporting

If you experience issues, open a private report through the repository maintainers.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove contributions or restrict participation when behavior harms the community.
